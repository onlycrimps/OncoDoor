About Us

Who We Are

At OncoDoor, we’re a small, agile team passionate about keeping oncology door systems running smoothly. In your clinic, the treatment machine is critical — and the door is right behind it. It has to operate flawlessly hundreds of times every day, and we’re here to make sure it does.

Why We Started

OncoDoor was born from the challenges we saw clinics face, especially in rural areas: costly new door installations and limited options for effective retrofits or maintenance. With decades of experience as product engineers specializing in mechanical drivetrains and control systems, we’re able to create practical, custom solutions quickly.

Our Mission

Based in Tulsa, OK, we’re proud to serve our local medical community with integrity and engineering precision. Our goal is simple: help clinics run reliably so that neighbors, friends, and families can get the treatment they need — every day, without interruptions.

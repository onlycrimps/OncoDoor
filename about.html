About OncoDoor

OncoDoor was founded to provide specialized, high-quality service for oncology door systems. Our founder brings over 10 years of engineering experience in product design, continuous improvement, and software integration — all applied to the critical components of medical infrastructure.

Our team is experienced in oncology facility needs and takes a patient-first approach: we prioritize safety, cost-effectiveness, and operational quality.

Based in Tulsa, OK, we’re proud to serve our local medical community with integrity and engineering precision.

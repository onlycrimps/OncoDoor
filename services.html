Services


Here is a list of services we provide — not exhaustive. If you’re unsure what your clinic may need, we offer a discovery session, including a site visit, to thoroughly inspect your doors and provide the best recommendations.




Preventative Maintenance Plans

At OncoDoor, we offer tiered preventative maintenance plans designed to keep your clinic’s doors operating reliably, day after day. Regular maintenance is the key to a long-life door — it prevents unexpected failures, reduces wear on critical components, and ensures doors continue functioning flawlessly hundreds of times daily.


  • Tiered Options: Flexible levels to fit your clinic’s needs and budget.

  • Predictive Reliability: Track door performance and replace critical parts before they fail, minimizing downtime.

  • Superior Safety & Efficiency: Simple, long-term solutions that reduce future maintenance costs and prevent operational errors.

  • Fast Local Response: Being Tulsa-based, we respond quickly when issues arise — no long waits or corporate delays.

With OncoDoor’s preventative maintenance, your doors stay safe, dependable, and long-lasting — protecting both your staff and your patients.

Repair & Replace

When a door fails, every minute counts.


  • Hard-to-Find Parts: We source specialized components that are often discontinued or unavailable from the OEM.

  • In House Developed Software-Driven Diagnostics: Our expertise allow us to efficiently troubleshoot larger or more complex failures.

  • Proactive Inventory: With certain service plans, we keep critical parts (unique to your clinic) on-hand to quickly respond to failures.

  • Intimate Door Knowledge: Our team gets to know your doors and anticipates issues before they disrupt operations.

Custom Solutions

When standard parts are unavailable or a complete new door operator isn’t practical, our team provides tailored, one-off solutions. We modify or upgrade software, sensors, motors, and mechanical components to improve reliability without breaking your budget.


  • One-Off Solutions: Custom modifications for unique situations where standard replacements aren’t feasible.

  • Software & Control Upgrades: Adjustments to door control logic to improve performance and reliability.

  • Mechanical & Sensor Adjustments: Replace or retrofit components to restore function without full system replacement.

  • Cost-Effective Reliability: We focus on improving system performance while keeping expenses reasonable.

  • Expert Engineering: Our team leverages decades of experience to design solutions that work and last.

Quality & Integrity

We deliver work you can trust. Every solution is built with precision, reliability, and long-term performance in mind. We are fully insured and always comply with local and state regulations — because your clinic deserves doors that work flawlessly, every day.

Service Area

Based in Tulsa, we provide fast, local support to clinics across Oklahoma, Kansas, Arkansas, and Missouri. Wherever you are in the region, we’re nearby to keep your doors running reliably and safely.
